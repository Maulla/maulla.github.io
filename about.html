About

Matthew Aull was raised in the highlands of New Jersey which has been a heavy source of inspiration in his work.

His interests include trail running, photography, and baking. Is anything better than a fresh loaf of bread? No.

Across his interests, repetition is played a large role, allowing an intimacy with a place or object that otherwise is one dimensional

Professionally, Matthew strives to provide access to information without barriers.
